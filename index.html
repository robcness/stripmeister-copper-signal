Strip value delta

Recycling-community terminology distinguishes insulated copper wire (jacketed, lower-yield) from #1 bare bright copper (clean, uncoated, unalloyed wire thicker than 16 gauge — the top-paid recovery grade). This widget treats the difference between the two as a reference raw-material spread, not a guaranteed yard price. Local scrap yards quote daily and apply their own grading; confirm with the buyer before committing volume.
